Driving is Your Living

As a minicab driver, a car’s more than just a way to get around. It’s your livelihood. We know this because we’re one of the only car supermarkets that offer finance to minicab drivers. With a range of competitive Finance Deals our team are available 7days a week. We’ll take extra care to offer you the best payment plans. We offer flexible terms, from 36 to 60 months. Not to mention fixed monthly payments to help you manage your cash flow.

Our simple online application makes the process easy and our multi-award winning Finance Advisors will support you through the process from start to finish.

Hassle Free Finance Application

You can apply for finance online or visit our dedicated Finance Managers in our showroom who will guide you through your application.

They understand your business and will gladly discuss your needs. If everything’s in order an agreement could be reached.
